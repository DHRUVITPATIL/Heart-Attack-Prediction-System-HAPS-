About Heart Attack Prediction System (HAPS)

Heart Attack Prediction System (HAPS) is a modern web application that leverages the power of machine learning to help users assess their risk of heart disease. Our mission is to make health insights accessible, fast, and user-friendlyâ€”empowering users to make informed decisions about their well-being.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and easy-to-use predictive tools for everyone, regardless of technical background. We believe technology can save lives by providing early risk warnings and promoting preventive care.

How it Works

  • Users fill out a simple form with 13 standard health attributes.
  • Our platform analyzes the input using advanced machine learning algorithms trained on real medical data.
  • An instant prediction is provided, indicating the likelihood of heart disease.

Disclaimer

HAPS is intended for informational purposes only. It cannot replace professional medical diagnosis. Always consult your doctor for concrete health concerns.
